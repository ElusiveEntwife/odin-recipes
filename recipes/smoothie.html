Berry Banana Smoothie

Antioxident-filled Berry Banana Smoothie

Are you craving a sweet treat? This purple smoothie has all the creamy and fruity flavors that you're looking
for. Not only is this smoothie easy, tasty, and pretty, it's filled with essential nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 3/4 cup greek yogurt
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Peel and add banana to blender.
  2. Add your frozen berries and spinach.
  3. Add the greek yogurt and a few splashes of milk.
  4. Blend on high adding milk to your preffered consistency.
  5. Serve in a tall glass and enjoy.
