Homemade Chipotle Bowls

Ground Beef Chipotle Bowls

If you're tempted to go out to get Chipotle, consider making this simple and healthy option. With just
a few ingredients, you'll have Chipotle in the comfort of your own home!

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1/4 cup green salsa
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 can no sodium black beans
  • Monteray jack cheese
  • Salt
Optional
  • Queso blanco
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Start boiling your rice according to box instructions.
  2. Place black beans in a small pot to simmer.
  3. Add ground beef to a pan and turn to medium-high heat.
  4. Once the beef has lost all it's pink color, add your green salsa and continue cooking on
    high heat
  5. When the beef has begun browning and criping at the bottom of the pan remove from heat.
  6. In a bowl layer your rice, cheese, beans, and beef.
  7. Add your optional queso and hot sauce.
  8. Serve and enjoy with tortilla chips.
