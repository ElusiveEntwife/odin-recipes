Noodle Stirfry

Delicious Asian Noodle Stirfry

If you're craving chinese takeout but want to make a healthier version at home, this recipe is for you. Some
pantry staples, along with a few extra ingredients, make this recipe cheap, easy, and tasty.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • 5 broccoli florets
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
Optional
  • 1 tablespoon sweet chili sauce

Steps

  1. Start boiling a serving of spaghetti noodles in a medium pot following the box instructions.
  2. Add your olive oil and sesame oil to a pan. Turn to medium-high heat.
  3. Add chili flakes, garlic, and ginger to the pan and cook until fragrant.
  4. Tear apart your broccoli florets into bite size pieces, and add to the pan. Turn to medium heat and stir occasionally.
  5. Next, mix soy sauce, sugar, oyster sauce, and the optionl sweet chili in a bowl.
  6. While the broccoli is cooking, crack 2 eggs into a bowl and whisk until scrambled.
  7. Once broccoli is cooked to your liking, add the egg.
  8. Now, add your sauce mixture and stir making sure to incorporate it into all the broccoli and egg.
  9. By this point your noodles should be done, and you can add them to the pan.
  10. Mix everything well. Let the noodles stirfry in the pan to soak up all of the flavors.
  11. Finally, plate up your meal and enjoy!
